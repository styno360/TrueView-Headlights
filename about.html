OEM restoration and refinishing by Jayden Fierbach — Kamloops, BC.

Specialized. Built for Durability.

My name is Jayden Fierbach, and I specialize exclusively in OEM headlights. This isn’t a side service added to detailing — headlights are the sole focus. I source, restore, and refinish OEM units for customers across Canada and the United States.

I began restoring headlights after noticing how frequently full replacement was recommended when proper restoration was still possible. Many common methods — quick polish jobs, wipe-on coatings, or single-stage sanding — looked good initially but failed within months.

I tested multiple sanding systems, preparation methods, and refinishing approaches before developing a structured process I trust. The goal was never speed.

It was longevity.
