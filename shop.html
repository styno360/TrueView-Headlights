How Inventory Works

Sourced From Salvage

OEM headlights are acquired from scrap and salvage vehicles. Inventory depends entirely on availability — specific models cannot always be sourced on demand.

Professionally Restored

Each unit undergoes a structured two-step process: multi-stage sanding to remove oxidation, followed by UV-resistant protection for long-term clarity.

Listed When Ready

Units are posted to eBay and Facebook Marketplace once fully restored. When they’re sold, they’re gone.
